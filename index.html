Professional

For eight years, I worked in the consumer banking industry. My roles covered branch management, lending, retirement planning, day to day transactions, and operations. While I was successful in my field, I knew it wasn't my calling. So I took the plunge to go back to school and reboot my career. I found Eleven Fifty Academy and joined their Javascript program to transition into web development, and I am loving it.

Personal

I live in Noblesville with my wife, Brenna, my two-year-old son, Desmond, and our cockapoo, Alfie. I first moved to Indiana to attend Taylor University where I graduated magna cum laude with a degree in international business. I have lived in seven states and four countries, so it's hard to tell you where I'm from, but Indiana is home to me now.
