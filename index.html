About Me

For nearly ten years, I worked in the consumer banking industry. My roles covered lending, retirement planning, day to day transactions, branch operations, and managing a team. After all that time, however, it was time for a change. I was good at it, but I just didn't love it. I found Eleven Fifty Academy and made the big choice to hit reset on my career. I couldn't be happier with that choice.
I live in Noblesville with my wife, Brenna, my two-year-old son, Desmond, and our cockapoo, Alfie. I've lived in seven states and four countries, so it's hard to tell you where I'm from, but Indiana is home to me now.
I have been involved with music and theatre my whole life. I still perform in several community theatres in the area whenever I can. I even met my wife at the Belfry Theatre in Noblesville. I'm a huge scifi/fantasy nerd, specializing in Doctor Who, Star Trek: TNG, Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, and Harry Potter (and plenty more.)
